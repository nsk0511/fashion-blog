Introduction to Fashion

Fashion is a powerful form of self-expression that allows people to showcase their personality and creativity through clothing and accessories. Over the years fashion has evolved from traditional clothing styles to modern global trends influenced by designers and social media.

The fashion industry includes clothing design, textile production, marketing and fashion retail businesses. Fashion trends change frequently and are influenced by culture, technology and lifestyle.
